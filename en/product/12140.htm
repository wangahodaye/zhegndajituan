Entecavir Dispersible Tablets

0.5mg ; 1mg

Indications

Entecavir Dispersible Tablets are indicated for the treatment of chronic hepatitis B in adults with evidence of active viral replication and either evidence of persistent elevations in serum aminotransferases (ALT ) or histologically active disease.
